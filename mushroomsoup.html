Creamy Mushroom soup!

this is my girlfriends favorite dish!

all you need for this amazing creamy mouth watering goodness is some love, care and good sweet old time!

Ingredients!

  • 4 tbs extra virgin olive oil
  • 4 tbs butter
  • 2 pounds (900g) cremini mushrooms
  • handful shiitake mushrooms
  • 3/4 cup minced shallots
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tps salt
  • 4 cups chicken stock
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
