this here is the greatest tuna dish you can make at home! its called ahi tuna steak!

all you need is to marinade the steak is some sesame oil, soy sauce, fresh ginger, garlic and fresh lime juice if you scroll down you will have the full recipe with right ammounts of ingredients :)

For the marinade!

  • 2 tablespoons toasted sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh lime juice

    • Then the steaks!

      1. heat up a pan on high heat!
      2. sear the steaks for 45 seconds each side!
      3. take the steaks off the heat hit them with some salt and leave them to rest!for a 5 minutes
      4. cut the steaks in to 2-3 cm slices and serve with your favorite side dish!
